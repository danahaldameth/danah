Danah Aldameth

Danah Aldameth is a full time student majoring in Graphic Design at Seminole State College and University of Central Floirda. She focuses with interests in branding and marketing and how they correlate to Graphic Design. Previously a Yearbook staff member for 4 years, she worked on editorials and page design as well as writing and editing copies. She aims to challenge design and put a perspective out on what Graphic Design could be. She has created graphics for Seminole County departments and was President of an online newspaper that dealt in web design and working on articles as well as editing them. She has taken courses and been official certified in Adobe Software such as Photoshop and InDesign.
