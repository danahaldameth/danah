Caring for your kittens fur

The best ways to care for fur of your kitten without harsh chemicals.When purchasing products to care for your cats fur, consider the type of fur your cat has, for example, longer fur needs more grooming. Always purchase paraben free and non acidic products. Remember to use brushes made from non toxic material as well. Shampoos and conditioners can keep your kittens fur shiny and clean, but always check the ingredients, and make sure you're using all natural products.

Safe foods for your cat

Foods that won't harm your kitten and are safe for their digestive systems This topic is subjective on the matter of specific felines as some breeds require more protein and cats in general have differnt tastes. Always go for wet foods soaked in water rather than oil, and for dry foods, focus on ones that advertise to help teeth. Make sure you get good food for your cat as that will result in less trips to the vet. For what you spend on food, you will save on vet bills. Choose organic brands of fufilling fish, and meats. Cats prefer more seafood-esque wet foods as it is more common to their natural core.

The best and safest litter

Litter can be a difficult choice but with this guide you can easily choose which one best fits your lifestyle.Some litters last longer, but that all depends how often you will be home to change it. Choose a litter that best reflects how often you will realisticlly change the litter. Ideally you would want to change it once a week, for a one cat household. To eleminate further orders, sprinkle some baking soda after each litter change to ensure more freshness. Remember to get a litter box that will fit your cat well so there isnt any excess litter around the area that would draw in bacteria.
